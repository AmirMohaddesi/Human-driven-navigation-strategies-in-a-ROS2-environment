Advanced Topics

Map Merging and Coordination (Upcoming)

Future updates will include capabilities for merging maps from multiple robots into a single global map. This will enhance coordination and allow robots to effectively share exploration data and navigation tasks.

Heterogeneous Robot Integration (Upcoming)

Support for integrating different types of robots (e.g., drones, ground vehicles, manipulators) will be provided, enabling diverse robotic teams to collaborate effectively in complex scenarios.

Deployment on Physical Robots (Future Plans)

Our ultimate goal includes seamless transition from simulation to deployment on real robotic hardware, handling network communication, sensor data, and environmental unpredictability effectively.

Enhanced User Interface

Future releases will introduce user-friendly graphical interfaces and dashboards to simplify system configuration, operation monitoring, and multi-agent management.

Performance Optimization

As the scale of robot deployment increases, performance optimization through efficient resource management and optimized ROS2 configurations will be prioritized to maintain system responsiveness and reliability.
